Jarvis

Jarvis helps a creator prepare, review, and submit short-form video posts for their own connected TikTok account.

The service is built around explicit user authorization: the account owner connects TikTok, reviews the selected media and caption, then intentionally submits the post workflow.

Account owner controlled Jarvis is used by the authorized account owner for their own creator or business posting workflow.
Review before submit Media, captions, privacy choices, and submission details are shown before the final action.
Platform-aware posting The integration uses TikTok authorization and Content Posting API capabilities only for approved posting tasks.

How Jarvis Works

Jarvis keeps the posting flow simple and auditable. A user connects their own account, selects a prepared video asset, enters or confirms the caption, checks posting options, and submits the final publishing action.

Connect TikTok through the official authorization flow.
Prepare a video post with user-selected media and caption text.
Review account capability, posting options, and content details.
Submit or test the post through the approved integration.
