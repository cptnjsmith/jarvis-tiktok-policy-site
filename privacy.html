Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Jarvis TikTok posting integration may collect, use, and store limited information necessary to support TikTok authorization and content posting workflows.

Information We May Collect

How Information Is Used

Information is used only to support authorized TikTok posting workflows, account connection, content preparation, and related operational tasks.

Sharing of Information

Information is not sold. Information may be shared only as necessary to interact with TikTok services or to comply with legal obligations.

Data Retention

Information is retained only as long as reasonably necessary for operational use, security, compliance, or troubleshooting.

Security

Reasonable steps are taken to protect stored credentials and related account information, but no system can be guaranteed perfectly secure.

Your Choices

You may revoke TikTok authorization or discontinue use of the integration at any time, subject to platform rules and any retained compliance obligations.

Contact
