Jarvis Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Jarvis handles information when a user connects a TikTok account they are authorized to use and uses Jarvis to prepare, review, and submit short-form video posts.

Information Jarvis May Process

  • Basic account information returned by TikTok after the user authorizes Jarvis.
  • OAuth access tokens, refresh tokens, open ID values, scopes, token expiry times, and related authorization metadata.
  • Creator posting capability information returned by TikTok, such as available privacy levels or posting limits.
  • Video file references, captions, posting options, publish identifiers, and status responses needed to prepare or submit content.
  • Operational logs needed to troubleshoot authorization, upload, or publishing issues.

How Information Is Used

Jarvis uses this information only to provide the user-authorized TikTok posting workflow: account connection, creator capability checks, content preparation, upload initialization, upload handling, publish submission, and publish status checks.

Sharing of Information

Jarvis does not sell personal information. Information is shared with TikTok only as needed to operate the authorized integration, or when required to comply with law, security obligations, or platform requirements.

Retention

Jarvis retains authorization and posting workflow information only as long as reasonably needed for operation, troubleshooting, audit, security, and compliance. Users may revoke TikTok authorization at any time through TikTok account settings or by discontinuing use of Jarvis.

Security

Jarvis is designed to keep credentials and account information limited to the authorized workflow. No internet-connected system can be guaranteed perfectly secure, but reasonable safeguards are used to limit access to credentials and posting data.

User Choices

Users control whether to connect their TikTok account, what content to prepare, and whether to submit a post. Users can revoke access in TikTok and request support through the contact method below.

Contact

For privacy questions or access requests, contact Jarvis support through the cptnjsmith GitHub profile.
